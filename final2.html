New York State: Effects on Electric Production Due to Renewable Sources
For more than 20 years, New York State has had continuous plans to lessen its dependence on non-renewable energy sources. Starting in 2000 with the introduction of rebates for solar renewable installations, NYSERDA began tracking the impact of renewable energy production (gigawatt hours, "GWH") against overall energy consumption.
